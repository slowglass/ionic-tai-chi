Yang Cheng Fu's 10 Principles

虚灵顶劲
Suspend the head from the sky
含胸拔背
Sink the chest, open the upper back
松腰
Loosen the waist
沉肩坠肘
Sink the shoulders, drop the elbows
分虚实
Distinguish between full and empty
用意不用力
Use will, not strength
上下相随
Co-ordinate the upper and lower body
内外相合
Unify external and internal movements
相连不断
Unbroken continuity of flow
动中求静
Seek stillness in movement
